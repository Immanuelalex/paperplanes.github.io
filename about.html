PAPER PLANES Design Studio is an up and coming architectural studio, focused on delivering quality designs, pertaining to the budget and producing holistic design solutions to the client

About the founder

Madhumitha Sekar completed her B.Arch. in School of Architecture and Planning, Chennai at 2019 following which she worked for a year in Midori Architects, Chennai. She decided to start Paper planes as an ode to design, which would act as a bridge between affordable sustainable design and clients, who have a dream project. Combined with her experience in internship in Kerala, and energy efficient residences worked on in her Chennai tenure, she became a certified Indian Green Building Council Accredited Professional(IGBC AP), and GRIHA GA in 2020

Established at 2020, the studio offers the following services:

  • Architecture
  • Interior Design
  • Landscape Design
  • 3D Visualisation
  • Furniture Design
