PAVENDHER RESIDENCE, Erode

Category: Residential

Area : 2200 Sq.Ft

Year : 2019

Location: Erode

  • Description
  • Related Images

This Brick residence was a collaborative effort with constant ideation by the client who wanted a minimalistic exposed brick residence A G+ 1 Structure with a double height living space and guest rooms in the ground floor, the master suite was designed in the first floor, each provided with a large balcony space Wooden rafters, and steel bars were reused from their existing building, and the foundation was left untouched and planned around it Large circular windows, dome roof, Polished concrete flooring, and lime plaster of light hues as the feature wall, the project exudes simplicity in all aspects of the project Rainwater harvesting system, Recycled water from STP used for Landscaping, low emitting Fixtures, are few of the elements incorporated for their hopes of a sustainable lifestyle
