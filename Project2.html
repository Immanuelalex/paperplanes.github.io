HIBISCUS FARM HOUSE, Pondicherry

Category: Residential

Area : 1500 Sq.Ft

Year : 2019

Location: Pondicherry

  • Description
  • Related Images

A Retreat home, which doubles up as a home stay for tourists in Pondicherry, this project can have a mixed use for a single family of four, or 3 tourist groups with no corelation to each other. Currently designed for single storey, with hopes of expanding for two storey with a pool based on future demands, the current design is based on simplicity in design, and multiple landscaping elements around the site. Bamboo poles, and Jaali tiles utilised in the elevation, to bring in a contemporary feel to the project. Long windows, a characteristic of Auroville architecture has been incorporated in the project. Filler slabs, Wire cut bricks, Cavity roofing tiles, Mixture of kota stones, Athangudi tiles, and lime plastering are few of the cultural elements integrated in this project
