BHASKAR RESIDENCE, Erode

Category: Residential

Area : 3200 Sq.Ft

Year : 2020

Location: Erode

  • Description
  • Related Images

A tightly packed plot of 2275 sq. Ft provided a requirement of careful consideration on planning the residence to give an outcome of clearly designated space with appropriate organisation of served and servant spaces, I.E circulation and living spaces This double storied residence in Erode features typical floors and set back storeys, with lush vegetation moved to terrace which acts as the buffer space for the residence. The block is split in two with clear distinction between public and private spaces.

Use of Porotherm block, Terracotta clay tiles, and Aerocon Blocks for good ventilation, the interiors are a stark contrast with clean contemporary spaces according to the usage. Equipped with proper structural members, this building can also be adapted for future usage and increased requirement of built-up area
