MAHILAM RESIDENCE, Erode

Category: Residential

Area : 4000 Sq.Ft

Year : 2019

Location: Erode

  • Description
  • Related Images

A Retreat home in Perundurai for a family of five, this project was planned around the existing mango tree which is designed as the entry seater The colour palette consists of white, black and grey for the exteriors, with recycled wooden panelling for the interior feature walls Materials are relatively low cost but robust, and interior spaces efficiently arranged around a central to minimize circulation space and maximize the available interior and exterior private open space The sustainability of the project is achieved in the efficiency of its plot use, balancing solid and void to ensure that units are spacious but efficient Materials are generally locally sourced and produced, minimizing energy expenditure Natural ventilation is promoted with the extensive openable glazing to facades and balconies
